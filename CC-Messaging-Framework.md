# Counter Cultures — Messaging Framework

**The Rule:** If someone lands on any page and doesn't understand what Counter Cultures does within 10 seconds, we've failed. Every page should reinforce who we are, what we sell, where we are, and why we're different.

---

## The Core Message (say this everywhere, different ways)

**What:** Premium kitchen, bath, and architectural hardware
**Where:** San Miguel de Allende, Mexico
**How:** Curated international brands + Mexican artisans, one showroom
**Why different:** Quality over quantity. Every piece is chosen, not just stocked.
**Promise:** We find it, order it, and deliver it — so you don't have to figure out international sourcing.

## Page-by-Page Recommendations

### Homepage
**Current problem:** Hero says "Smart Bathrooms / Smart Toilets" — could be any company, anywhere.

**Fix:** New hero (see options above) + add a one-liner subhead that says exactly what CC does and where. Below the hero, the brand bar is good but needs a label: "Authorized dealer for the world's leading brands."

The "Shop by Category" section should say "Browse our collection" with a line like: "491 premium pieces across kitchen, bath, and hardware — all available for order and delivery in Mexico."

### Product Pages
**Current problem:** Products show price and image but don't reinforce who's selling them.

**Fix:** Every product page should include:
- "Available at Counter Cultures, San Miguel de Allende"
- "Authorized [Brand] dealer"
- Delivery estimate: "Typical delivery: [X] weeks to your project site"
- A CTA that says what happens next: "Interested? We'll get you a quote within 24 hours."

### Brand Pages
**Current problem:** Good copy about the brands, but stock images and no connection to Counter Cultures.

**Fix:** Each brand page should open with: "Counter Cultures is an authorized [Brand] dealer in San Miguel de Allende." Add a line about what CC carries from that brand: "We carry [X] pieces from [Brand]'s [collection names] collections."

### About/Our Story
**Current problem:** Good founder narrative, but buries the "what we do" too deep.

**Fix:** First sentence should be: "Counter Cultures is a premium kitchen, bath, and architectural hardware showroom in San Miguel de Allende, Mexico." Then the founder story. End with the quality-over-quantity philosophy.

### Footer (every page)
**Current problem:** Contact info is there but no positioning statement.

**Fix:** Add a one-liner above the footer links: "Premium kitchen, bath & hardware — San Miguel de Allende, Mexico" — this way even if someone scrolls to the bottom without reading anything, they get it.

### Contact/CTA Sections
**Current problem:** "Start Your Project" is good but vague.

**Fix:** "Start Your Project" + a subline: "Tell us what you're looking for. We'll find it, quote it, and deliver it." This tells them exactly what happens when they reach out.

## The Delivery Promise (needs Roger's input)

One of the biggest gaps: the site never tells you how fast things happen. For a business where you're ordering from international manufacturers, people want to know:

- **How fast do you respond?** → "We respond to every inquiry within 24 hours"
- **How fast do you quote?** → "Quotes within [X] business days"
- **How long does delivery take?** → "Most orders delivered in [X-Y] weeks" (this varies by brand — Roger needs to give us ranges)
- **Do you ship nationwide?** → "Delivery across Mexico" or "Delivery to San Miguel de Allende and surrounding areas"

Roger: we need these numbers from you. They go everywhere — product pages, the contact page, the trade program page, the footer.
